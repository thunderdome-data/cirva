Disability claims pile up at Department of Veterans Affairs offices
Veterans submitting disability claims to Veterans Affairs have to wait for long stretches — sometimes more than a year — for their applications to be processed. The Center for Investigative Reporting analyzed data collected from Freedom of Information Act requests and the Department of Veterans Affairs. You can explore this data to see how many cases were waiting to be processed at each of the VA's 58 regional offices in recent years compared to the national average.
